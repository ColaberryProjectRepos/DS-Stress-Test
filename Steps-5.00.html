Message from the General:

Being in the C.O.E. programs are the quickest way to become an expert in your tool.  We are taking the days and weeks that it took to build this project and giving it to you in one of the clearest forms possible so you can complete it in minutes. You have the ability to rebuild this entire project by following pictures and videos. How amazing is that?  Each project you build is a project of experience.  The difference between an architect and a developer is the number of projects you've been exposed to. Increased exposure enhances your vision of the bigger picture which allows you to very quickly transition to the next level. 

 

What to critique: 

Your goal here is to complete the Step by Step instructions to build the project.  There will be obstacles.  There will be mistakes.  There may be parts that are very confusing, maybe even stopping you from moving forward.  That's the trade off here.  You get the chance to complete a project in a condensed amount of time and the person that built the project gets help quickly working out the bugs, moving them closer to getting it approved in the Colaberry Network.

 

How to critique:

Access the Project Link at the top of the post while logged into your student account in app.colaberry.com. You will see a similar screen.

 

Steps: Navigate through the steps requested by either clicking on the numbers at the top of the step  or clicking on the "Next Step" button at the bottom of the step. 

 

Adding Disputes: When you see any type of issue whether it be a spelling mistake or misinterpretation you will click on the dispute button to add a dispute.  The dispute screen looks like below.
